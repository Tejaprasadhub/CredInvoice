Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers.

What is invoice discounting? +

Invoice discounting is a financial tool where a business sells its unpaid invoices to a third party (a financier) at a discount to access immediate cash, rather than waiting for the standard payment term to end.

Who is eligible to use cred invoice? +

All legitimate business entities, including HUF, LLP, Partnership, Proprietorship, Trust, and Private Limited companies, are eligible to join after completing our mandatory KYC verification process.

Is it mandatory for buyers to use financiers? +

No. Buyers have the flexibility to fund discounted invoices using their own funds or to forward the invoice to our network of financiers to receive competitive bids for funding.

What are the costs associated with using the platform? +

Our platform operates on a commission-based model. We believe in complete transparency. For a detailed breakdown of our fee structure, please visit our 'Pricing' page or contact our sales team for a personalized consultation.
